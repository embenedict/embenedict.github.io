Hi. I'm Betsy.

But a young sapling in photography, I eagerly search for images that could be my next favorite thing, and happiness in a person's face is an irresistible subject. I find happiness in scenery, too, and ethereal lighting (if I should be so lucky to capture that). Happiness is a feeling, but it is also an image... I hope to show my excitement for the simple, lovely feeling here, depicted in my photos.


Who is the girl behind the lens?

I'm originally from the flatlands of Indianapolis, IN. I attended Ball State University in the small-ish town Muncie, IN, where I studied nursing. Now I live in Berkeley, CA, where the weather is 60 degrees and sunny every day. I've enjoyed capturing the rural scenery that Indiana offers, and now look forward to framing the glamorous city of San Francisco and the Bay Area.


My website is a work in progress; everything you see I built from the ground-up, and I'd love your suggestions about how I could improve it to make it more fun! I don't have a comment box up yet, but don't let that stop you from sharing your brilliant ideas with me.

Go ahead, click around... and feel happier.
